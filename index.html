Types of Hacking

Phishing

Deceptive emails designed to steal your information.

Malware

Software designed to disrupt, damage, or gain unauthorized access to systems.

SQL Injection

Exploiting vulnerabilities in SQL databases.

Famous Hacking Incidents

Target Data Breach

In 2013, hackers stole 40 million credit and debit card records from Target.

Yahoo Data Breach

In 2014, a breach affected 500 million Yahoo accounts.

Protection Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Be cautious of suspicious emails and links.
